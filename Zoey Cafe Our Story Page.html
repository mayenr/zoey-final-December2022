Our Legacy

Zoey Café is a service-oriented organization that has been serving the people of Iligan City for almost 20 years. Zoey Café was created thru the idea of the booming coffee industry in the country. With this in mind, we wanted to seize the opportunity and so we made our aspiration into a reality. Thus, we celebrated the birth of Zoey Café on July 8, 2002.

In early 2013, we changed owner ship from sole proprietor to partner-ship and was named, Zoey Café, Co.

And so in June 8, 2013 we opened our first branch in Ubaldo Laya, Iligan City.
