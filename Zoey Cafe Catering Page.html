Our Catering

We offer catering services to all kinds of events and occasions:

  • All kinds of parties, events and occassions
  • Christening parties
  • Wedding parties - intimate or large weddings
  • Birthday parties
  • Anniversary parties - whether private or corporate
  • Holiday parties - Christmas & New Year parties, Valentines & Easter Celebration, etc.
  • Seminars and Meetings - we can hold your seminars and/or office and corporate meetings and provide the catering
  • Trainings and Lectures - we can hold your Trainings & Lecture and provide the venue and meals
  • Grazing Table - our Grazing Table offers an assortment of delicacies that you find in Grazing Boxes (e.g. imported cheese, bologna, caviar, etc.)
  • Grazing Boxes - we can prepare Grazing Boxes that you can give away to guests and peers
  • Packed Lunches - our Packed Lunches can be given away for events where they can bring home their meals instead

Please book an appointment ahead of time so we can attend to every detail of your needs. We aim to provide the best service.
